Bhagwan Shiv, or Shiva, is a Hindu deity who plays many roles, including the creator, protector, and transformer of the universe. He is also known as the great ascetic, the master of fertility, poison, and medicine, and the Lord of Cattle. In the Shakta tradition, the Supreme Goddess (Devi) is considered Shiva's equal complementary partner Shiva is part of the Hindu trinity,

the Trimurti, along with Brahma and Vishnu. In the Smarta tradition,     Shiva is one of five equivalent deities in Panchayatana puja.
