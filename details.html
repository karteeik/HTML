what you say about mahadev?

Mahadev is a title of respect for the Hindu god Shiva, which means "the great god" in Sanskrit. Shiva is considered the supreme being in Hinduism and a member of the Trimurti, the Hindu trinity that also includes Brahma and Vishnu. Shiva is known as the "Destroyer" in the Trimurti and is also revered as a symbol of transformation
